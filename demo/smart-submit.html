How Smart Submit Works

Both forms look identical. The user types the same real data in both. But the right form has sf-domain="banco.com" while we're on localhost — SmartField detects the domain mismatch (phishing) and sends fake encrypted data instead.

The hacker decrypts the payload and gets realistic-looking but completely fake data. They think they stole real credentials. They didn't.
